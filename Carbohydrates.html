Carbohydrates

Elements: C, H, O

- 1:2:1 ratio of C:H:O

Monomer: Monosaccharide

- Ex. Glucose, Fructose, Galactose

Two Monosaccharides: Disaccharide

- Bond: Glycosidic Linkage

- Ex. Surcrose, Lactose, Maltose

Polymer: Polysaccharide

- Structural: Cellulose (plant cell walls), Chitin (exoskeleton of arthropods)

- Storage: Starch (plants), Glycogen (animals)
